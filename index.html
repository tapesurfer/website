About the Game

Ride the trends. Don't get liquidated.

Trust your instincts

A price line scrolls across the screen. You're on it. Stay right-side up if you think prices will rise, or flip upside down to profit when the market falls. Your goal is simple: maximize profits to finish the run. No real money, no real risk — just pure trading fantasy.

Levels from real market data

Dogecoin. Coffee. Bitcoin. Gold. Every level is built from a real price chart. The lines you're riding aren't random — they're history. Six tiers of real markets, each one wilder than the last.

Push your luck

Every time you switch sides, you finish a trade and start a new one. String profitable trades for a streak bonus. Hold the leverage button to crank up your risk — bigger rewards, bigger losses, bigger fees. Hit zero and you get liquidated.

Shape your run

Pick contracts between stages that shape your run: bigger bonuses, passive boosts, and powerful upgrades. Combine them for maximum profit. When you blow up, you wake up in a basement clinic — unlock new skills at the black market to change your playstyle. Go again.

About the Game

Every level is built from real historical price data.

Every level is a real market

Coffee futures. Bitcoin. Gold. Historic crashes. Six tiers of markets that actually happened — commodities, crypto, stocks, forex, bonds, and beyond. You're surfing real tape.

Read the tape or get liquidated

Pick a side — long or short. Read the price right and your balance grows. Read it wrong and commissions eat you alive. Chain winning trades into streaks for multiplied gains. Push too far and the liquidation engine kicks in. Hit zero and you're done.

Pick your style, build your deck

Master scalping and trend following. Predict moves as an oracle or nudge the price as an influencer. Between stages, draft cards — reactive events, passive buffs, and augments. Every pick reshapes how the market fights back.

Death is just the beginning

Death routes you through a recovery clinic. Talk to a darknet veteran and a burned-out quant. Choose your skills, pick your market, and go again. Every run is different.

Description

TapeSurfer is the stock market roguelite. Just like in real trading, you never know how the market will move. Trust your instincts to ride the trends: stay right-side up if you think prices will rise, or flip upside down to profit when the market falls. Your goal is simple: maximize profits to finish the run. No real money, no real risk — just pure trading fantasy.

Levels generated from real market data

Dogecoin. Coffee. Bitcoin. Gold. Every level is built from a real price chart. The lines you're riding aren't random — they're history.

Push your luck

Every time you switch sides, you finish a trade and start a new one. String profitable trades to win a streak bonus. Hold the leverage button to crank up your risk — bigger rewards, bigger losses, bigger fees. Hit zero and you get liquidated.

Shape your run

Pick contracts that shape your run. Bigger bonuses, passive boosts, and powerful upgrades. Combine them for maximum profit. Unlock new skills from the black market to change your trading playstyle.

Features

  • Real stock market and cryptocurrency charts
  • No real money, no real risk — just pure trading fantasy
  • Every run is different: new charts, unlockable contracts, and evolving trading playstyles
  • Build streaks of profitable trades for multiplied earnings, or get greedy and lose it all
  • Hold a button to increase your risk multiplier. Bigger wins, but bigger losses
  • Choose your path: each market you pick shapes the rest of your run

Description

TapeSurfer is a 2D side-scrolling roguelite where you ride real stock charts. A price line scrolls across the screen — jump above to bet it goes up, drop below to bet it goes down. Every level is built from real historical price data.

You ride the chart

A price line scrolls across the screen. You're on it. Jump above to bet the price goes up. Drop below to bet it goes down. Read it right and your money grows. Read it wrong and it drains fast. That's the core — read the chart, pick your side, and hang on.

Every level is a real market

Coffee futures. Bitcoin. Gold. Historic crashes. Every level uses actual historical price data — commodities, crypto, stocks, forex, and bonds. The charts you're riding actually happened. Six tiers of markets, each wilder than the last.

Chain combos, don't go broke

String correct calls together to build streaks that multiply your gains. Get greedy and push too hard, and you'll start bleeding money by the second. Hit zero and you're out. Every run is a balancing act between playing it safe and pushing your luck.

Draft cards, shape your run

Between stages, pick from a hand of cards that change how the game plays. Some spawn hazards on the chart. Others give you passive boosts or one-time power-ups. Each card reshapes the next level. When you die, you wake up in a basement clinic, talk to shady NPCs, unlock new skills, and go again.

Features

  • Real market data — every level uses actual historical price charts you ride through
  • Roguelite card drafting — pick cards between stages that add hazards, boosts, and power-ups
  • More than 15 levels across 6 tiers — from coffee futures to abstract void
  • Market events — flash crashes, whale dumps, squeezes, and rug pulls shake up the chart mid-run
  • Streak combos — chain correct calls for multiplied rewards, or overextend and lose it all
  • Hub world — a basement clinic and black market between runs for upgrades and story
  • Free-choice level select — pick which markets to tackle, each with escalating entry fees
